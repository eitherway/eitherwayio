The short version

I am using Simple Analytics as a way to see some statistics about my blog. However they don’t store or collect any personal data from you. For more information on what Simple Analytics collects (or mostly what they don’t collect) click here.

I am not using any tracking cookies nor fingerprinting technology.

To host this site, I am using Github Pages. Github collects personal information from you, including logs of your IP address, to comply with legal obligations, and to maintain the security and integrity of their Website and their Service.

If you contact me in any way (by phone, mail, e-mail), the data you provide will be saved and processed for handling your request. Without your consent, I will not transfer this data to anyone.

Legal basis

In the following privacy policy I communicate the legal basis of the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the German Federal Data Protection Act (BDSG), the German Telemedia Act (TMG), on the basis of which I process personal data.

Please note that in addition to the regulations of the GDPR, national data protection regulations may apply in your or my country of residence and domicile.

Provision of the online offer and web hosting

Collection of access data and log files: Github collects data on every access to the server. (so called logfiles). These data includes:

  • referrer URL (the previously visited page)
  • browser type and version
  • type of operating system
  • hostname of the computer
  • date and time of access
  • IP address

This data is not merged with other data sources. The legal basis for this is article 6 para. 1 lit. f GPDR. The Hosting Provider has a legitimate interest on maintaining the security and integrity of their Website and their Services. Therefore the server log files must be captured for this.


Deployed Service Provider: Github. Privacy Policy of Github. Github is a US processor certified under the Privacy Shield.

Contact Form or Requests per Phone, Mail or E-Mail

If you contact me by e-mail, mail or phone, your inquiry including all personal data (name, inquiry) will be stored and processed by me for the purpose of processing your request. I will not pass on this data without your consent.

This data is processed on the basis of Art. 6 para. 1 lit. b GDPR, if your inquiry is related to the fulfilment of a contract or is necessary for the implementation of pre-contractual measures. In all other cases, processing is based on my legitimate interest in the effective processing of the enquiries addressed to me (Art. 6 para. 1 lit. f GDPR) or on your consent (Art. 6 para. 1 lit. a GDPR), provided that the enquiry was made.

The data sent to me by you via contact enquiries remains with me until you request me to delete it, revoke your consent to its storage or the purpose for which it was stored ceases to apply (e.g. after your enquiry has been processed). Mandatory statutory provisions remain unaffected.

Deletion of Data

The data processed by me will be deleted in accordance with the legal requirements as soon as their consent permitted for processing is revoked or other permissions cease to apply (e.g. if the purpose for which the data were processed ceases to apply or if they are not necessary for the purpose).

Unless the data is deleted because they are required for other and legally permissible purposes, their processing is limited to these purposes. This means that the data is blocked and not processed for other purposes.
